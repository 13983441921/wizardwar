Privacy Policy

Orbital Labs, creator of Wizard War takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes what personal information we collect and how we use it. See this privacy policy primer to learn more about privacy policies in general.

Routine Information Collection

Our app collects basic information about your gameplay to provide a better user experience. This information includes frenemies (other players you have battled against in Multiplayer mode) win history, spells and quests unlocked, etc. The app also collects information about your location so that you can battle players near you. All of this information is stored locally on your phone. None of this information is stored on our servers and it can be deleted by deleting the app.

Facebook Connection

We use Facebook's secure oAuth connection to link your Wizard Profile to your Facebook account. This allows you to import your Facebook photo and invite Facebook friends to battle. None of your private account info is stored on our servers. Deleting the app will disconnect your Facebook account.

Contact Information

Concerns or questions about this privacy policy can be sent to support@wizardwarapp.com.
